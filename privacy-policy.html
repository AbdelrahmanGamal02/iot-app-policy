Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Unimog V-7993 ("the App"), developed by AbdelrahmanGamal02, handles information. The App is a mobile utility designed to allow users to configure and remotely control hardware relay boards.

2. Information We Collect and Process

The App is designed with privacy in mind. We minimize data collection to only what is necessary for functional hardware control:

  • Local Configuration Data: The App stores your added hardware boards, custom names, icons, and serial numbers locally on your device. This data never leaves your device unless sent to your configured MQTT broker.
  • Network Information: To perform Wi-Fi setup (SmartConfig) for your relay boards, the App requires access to network state details.
  • Camera Permission: The App asks for camera permission solely to scan QR codes on hardware boards for easy onboarding (serial number detection). Scanning is performed entirely on-device; no images are uploaded.

3. Third-Party Services and MQTT Broker

The App communicates with hardware boards using the MQTT protocol. You are responsible for configuring the MQTT broker endpoint. The data transmitted consists of control messages (e.g., turning a relay on or off) and status updates. We do not host or monitor your private MQTT traffic.

4. Data Sharing and Sale

We do not collect personal identity information, and we do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties.

5. Security

Since data is stored locally and sent over your designated MQTT broker, the security of your communication depends on your Wi-Fi network security and your MQTT broker credentials. We recommend using secure MQTT endpoints (MQTTS) and robust passwords.
